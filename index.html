Coffee cupcake with whipped cream

The perfect cake to go with your daily coffee. It’s very easy and on top of that it’s delicious, it’s sure to impress. Make it and then tell me what you think!

Time: 1h10
Yield: 12 servings
Difficulty: easy

Igredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups (tea) of warm water
  • 1 tablespoon (soup) of instant coffee
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup (tea) of oil
  • 2 cups (tea) of sugar
  • 2/3 cup (tea) of cocoa powder
  • 2 and 1/2 cups (tea) of wheat flour
  • 1 tablespoon (soup) of baking powder
  • 2 cups (tea) of ready-made whipped cream
  • Coffee beans for decoration
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon (soup) of instant coffee
  • 1 tablespoon (soup) of cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons (soup) of butter

Preparation method

In a blender, beat the water, instant coffee, eggs, oil, sugar and cocoa powder until smooth. Transfer to a bowl, add the flour and baking powder and mix with a spoon. Pour into cupcake molds lined with paper liners and place them side by side in a large pan. Bake in a preheated medium oven for 25 minutes or until cooked and golden. Remove and let cool.

For the filling, heat all the ingredients over medium heat, stirring well until thickened. Turn off the heat, let cool and place in a piping bag with a pearl tip. Set aside. Place the whipped cream in a piping bag with a star tip and set aside.

To assemble, make a hole in the center of the cupcakes and fill with the reserved coffee brigadeiro. Decorate the surfaces of the cupcakes with the reserved whipped cream, making circles. Decorate with coffee beans and refrigerate until ready to serve.
